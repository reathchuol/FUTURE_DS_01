# 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes business sales data using retail transaction dataset to uncover key insights that support strategic decision-making. The core objective of analysis is to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance and answer critical business questions.
The workflow combines Python (Jupyter Notebook) for data cleaning and transformation and Microsoft Power BI for interactive dashboard visualization. 

# 🎯 Key Business Questions 
This analysis focus on answering core business questions such as;
1.	Which Product generate the most revenue?
2.	How do Sales Change over time?
3.	Which regions are most profitable?
4.	Where should the business focus to grow faster?

The workflow combines Python (Jupyter Notebook) for data cleaning and transformation, and Microsoft Power BI for interactive dashboard development.

# Key Insights
# 1.	Top-Performing Products
According to the data, the top five (5) products that contributes much of the total revenue include the (PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BRIDIE), REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER, WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER, JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT, and MEDIUM CERAMIC TOP STORAGE JAR with (PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BRIDIE), and REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER respectively accounting for 1.89% and 1.60% of the total revenue. This highlights that these products represent the core drivers of business performance.  

_**Insight:**_  Revenue is concentrated within a small portfolio of products, making them critical drivers of overall business performance while also posing dependency risks.

# 2.	Sales Trends Over Time
Monthly sales begin at moderate levels (569,445.04 in January), experiences fluctuations mid-year, and then rises sharply from Month 8 (August) till Month 9 (September) and continuous to grow steadily, peaking at over 1.16M in Month 11 (November) before a slight decline in Month 12 (December). 

_**Insight:**_ Sales exhibit strong seasonal demand, with peak performance in the third quarter (Q3) and final quarter (Q4), likely tied to increased consumer spending during this period.

# 3.	Regional and Country Performance
The European Union (EU) region overwhelmingly dominates revenue generation, contributing about 8.68M from 24 countries, while other regions like Oceania (OC), Asia (AS), and North America (NA), Africa (AF) among others contribute only marginally. The United Kingdom in EU market accounts for the biggest revenue total of approximately 7.31M 

_**Insight:**_ The business is highly dependent on the a single region (EU market), with significant untapped potential in other regions including AF, AS, NA, and NA among others.

# 4.	Revenue Distribution
The data shows that some products generate higher revenue without necessarily having the highest sales volume, indicating the influence of pricing and perceived value. For example, REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER with a quantity of 12,402 generates 1.60% of the total revenue much greater than 0.15% of the total revenue generated by WORLD WAR 2 GLIDERS ASSID DESIGNS with a huge quantity of 54,415. 

_**Insight:**_ High-volume sales do not always equate to high revenue. Strategic pricing and product value have a greater impact on revenue than volume alone.

# Recommendation	
# 1.	Scale High-Performing Products 

Increase investment in inventory, marketing, and distribution for the top-performing products, particularly (PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BRIDIE), REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER, WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER. Introduce bundles and complementary product offerings to maximize revenue per transaction. 

# 2.	Leverage Seasonal Peaks 

Align marketing campaigns, promotions, and inventory planning with the strong Q3 and Q4 demand surge. Develop seasonal product variations and targeted campaigns to capitalize on peak buying periods.  

# 3.	Expand Geographical Reach 

Reduce overdependence on the EU market by expanding into underperforming regions such as North America, Asia, and Oceania. Tailor pricing, marketing strategies, and partnerships to local market dynamics.  Leverage high product demands by region. For example, manual in Asia, POSTAGE in North America, and REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER in South America. 

# 4.	Optimize Product Portfolio

Evaluate lower-performing products (such as CARTOON PENCIL SHARPENERS, PORCELAIN BUDAH INCENSE HOLDER, and HANGING WOOD AND FELT FLOWER) for improvement, repositioning, or discontinuation. Focus on products that demonstrate strong revenue efficiency and margin potential. 

# 5.	Refine Pricing and Segmentation Strategies 

Leverage insights from revenue vs. quantity patterns to optimize pricing models. Implement customer and regional segmentation to deliver targeted offers and improve conversion rates. Strong consideration on less volume products that generate much revenue.
